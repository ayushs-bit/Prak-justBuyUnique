Bulk Orders FAQs

1. Will I get a GST invoice for bulk purchases?

Yes. We share quotes only from those sellers who can give a GST invoice.

2. I want to purchase a product in bulk but the product page is showing a quantity limit. Can I purchase it in bulk?

For certain products, there are quantity limits set by the sellers to prevent abuse. For such requirements, we will get a special offer created by removing the quantity restrictions and you can make the purchase.

3. Can I purchase Prak Exclusive mobiles in bulk?

No, at present we do not have an option to buy Exclusive mobile brands in bulk.

4. Once I place an order, in how many days will I get the products?

We try to find sellers near your location to expedite the delivery process. However, if sellers are not available near your location, then the delivery timelines is subject to the seller location.
